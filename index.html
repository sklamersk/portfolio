01 — About

Get to know me

I'm a Computer Engineering graduate from the University of the Philippines – Diliman, with a background that spans both sides of the stack: embedded systems and hardware-software integration on one end, and applied AI / software development on the other. My thesis and coursework had me working with microcontrollers, FPGAs, and sensor pipelines, while my more recent projects have pulled me toward Python-based AI tooling and end-to-end system integration.

Lately, my focus has shifted toward no-code and low-code workflow automation — using tools like Zapier, Make, and Playwright to remove repetitive manual work from real operations. I like projects that sit at the intersection of "this should just run itself" and "this needs to actually be engineered correctly."

I'm currently actively looking for full-time opportunities in AI engineering, data engineering, QA/automation, or software engineering — and I'm always happy to talk shop about a role, a project, or both.
